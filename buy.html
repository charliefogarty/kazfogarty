WHAT WE BUY

Many people have jewelry they have inherited, received as a gift or purchased for themselves which they no longer wear. Tastes change, styles change and jewelry collections, like everything else, need editing or updating from time to time. If you have your eye on something else, or simply wish to realize the value in a piece of jewelry you currently own, we are dedicated to making the process as fair, straightforward, and uncomplicated as possible.


We purchase all jewelry with intrinsic value, from large collections of diamond, platinum and precious gem-set pieces to small gold charms. Very little is too small for us to purchase and nothing is too large. We buy every vintage from Georgian to Victorian, Edwardian, Art Nouveau, Art Deco, Retro 1940's, mid-century and modern designer pieces. We also pay the very highest prices for signed designer pieces including Cartier, Van Cleef and Arpels, Harry Winston, Bulgari, Tiffany and Co., David Webb, Verdura, and Graff, amongst many others. Please feel comfortable to discuss with us anything you may be considering selling. There is no charge whatsoever for our opinion or valuation.
